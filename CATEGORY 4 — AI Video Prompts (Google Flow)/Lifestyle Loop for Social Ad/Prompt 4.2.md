## **Prompt 4.2 — Lifestyle Loop for Social Ad**



SCENE: 3-second seamlessly looping video. Young professional 

opens a delivery app on their phone while sitting at a sunlit 

outdoor cafe table in Colombo. Camera angle: over-the-shoulder 

shallow depth of field. The moment loops: hand reaches for 

phone, taps screen, brief smile, repeat. 



Motion: slow motion at 60% speed. Colour grade: warm, 

slightly overexposed highlights, lifestyle Instagram aesthetic.

Loop point: perfectly matched first and last frame for 

seamless repeat. Designed for paid social ad placement.

No text overlay needed — leave screen visible.



LOOP STRUCTURE:

\- Frame 0: hand resting near phone

\- Frame 45: hand picks up phone  

\- Frame 90: app opens, expression reacts
