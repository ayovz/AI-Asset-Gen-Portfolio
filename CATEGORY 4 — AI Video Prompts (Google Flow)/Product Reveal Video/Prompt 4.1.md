## Prompt 4.1 — Product Reveal Video



SCENE: A premium smartphone rises slowly from below frame into the 

centre of a pure white infinite background. Motion: smooth, slow 

upward float at 0.5x natural speed. Lighting shifts from cool blue 

ambient to warm studio spotlight as device reaches centre frame. 

The phone rotates 45 degrees clockwise over 3 seconds. Screen 

activates with a soft glow revealing a colourful app interface. 

Camera remains static. Duration: 6 seconds. 

Mood: premium Apple-style product reveal. No text. No voiceover.



MOTION PARAMETERS:

\- Start: device below frame, dark

\- Mid (3s): device centred, lighting transition  

\- End (6s): device rotated, screen glowing

\- Easing: ease-in-out on all movements

\- Background: pure white, no movement
